ABOUT CLIENT SERVICE

THE MAISON

I'm Roshanak Marefat, the founder of ROMA Fashion Studio.

ROMA comes from my own name — and Studio means this is more than a clothing label. It's a creative space for design, and in the future, for education and styling too.

ROMA makes handmade, limited-edition garments. Each piece is artistic, personal, and never mass-produced. My customers are people who are tired of fast fashion — they want clothing with meaning, quality, and a story.

"Recoloring San Francisco: bringing cultural color and individuality to a city that tends to dress in grey and black."

My inspiration comes from my Iranian heritage — rich jewel-tone colors, intricate patterns, and craftsmanship that takes time. I draw from Persian rug patterns, tile work, embroidery, and mineral wealth of the ancient Silk Road.

My signature detail is the zebra stripe: bold, graphic, and woven into every collection as a lining or design accent.

THE STUDIO

ROMA is not only a clothing label — it is a working studio. A space for design, personalized styling, and in the future, education for emerging designers. Every piece begins and ends here, by hand.
